Caption

This exhibit explores how memes, visual and textual objects created, shared, edited, and reshared online, have become integral to how we communicate with one another on a societal level. Once a medium to make jokes on the early internet, they are now an everyday occurrence and something you can barely escape. Not only has their popularity exploded, however. Memes have become a common way to communicate information, bond with others, and are capable of changing alongside culture. Our exhibit intends to explore how memes have become a language of sorts.
